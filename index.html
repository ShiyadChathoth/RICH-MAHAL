The Meaning

A model home shaped by peace, family, and harmony.

Located near Koothuparamba in the Kannur district of Kerala, Rich Mahal Vengad stands as a symbol of peaceful living. The name "Rich Mahal" does not signify material wealth. It reflects a home made rich by joy, kindness, and togetherness.

Recognition

Android Pathootty brought a family innovation into public attention.

Muhammad Shiyad Chathoth, son of Abdul Rahman Vengad, gained widespread recognition for building Android Pathootty, a robot designed to assist his mother at home.

The innovation earned the prestigious Antonio Pellet Spanish Award and a special honor from the Honorable Chief Minister of Kerala, Pinarayi Vijayan.
